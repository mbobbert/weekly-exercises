Welcome to the Netherlands!

This is a website about Amsterdam. While most tourists come for the drugs, Amsterdam has much more to offer than weed and mushrooms. Think for example of the beautiful canals, the lovely tulip fields, the world famous museums and all the bicycles. In addition, it has some great national events, such as King's Day and Gay Pride. If you want to find out more about Amsterdam and it's upcoming events, please visit I Amsterdam.

Top 5 Reasons to visit Amsterdam

  1. The nice Canals
  2. The museums like Van Gogh, Rijksmuseum, and the Anne Frank museum
  3. The tulip fields
  4. The bicycle culture
  5. The great national festivals
